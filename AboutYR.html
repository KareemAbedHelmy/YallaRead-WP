About us
What is Yalla Read.

Yalla Read is a site made to help readers find books that will be enjoyable for the reader and suitable for their level.

Our targets.
  1. we target the new readers to help them to know the new books that they can read
  2. we give the reader links to books to make it easier to them.
About The Group Members

We are a group of prep year students in the BUE. This site was made as a part of a project
